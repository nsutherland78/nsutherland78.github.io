Playoff Rules

Instructions

At the beginning of the playoffs, participants must select the teams who they believe will win each of the best of 7 matchups in the Quarterfinals, and the number of games each team will win the matchup in (4, 5, 6 or 7 games).  This will only be done on a per round basis at the beginning of that round (not for the whole bracket).  Points for the correct number of games will only be awarded if the team they have chosen wins that matchup. 

Participants will also select the team that they believe will win the Stanley Cup (Final Round) at the beginning.  This chosen Stanley Cup ‘Champion’ cannot be modified once the playoffs begin.

Once the Quarterfinal round is complete, points will be tallied as per the below and carried forward to the next round. Once the new matchups are determined, participants will then select the teams who they believe will win each of the best of 7 matchups in the Semifinal round, and the number of games they will win the matchup in (4, 5, 6 or 7 games).

The same format will be followed for the Conference finals, and the Final.

Once the playoffs (Final round) are complete and the Stanley Cup winner is known, the sum of points for each participant from each round, and points for the correct Stanley Cup team will be tallied.  Participants will be awarded as per the ‘Point Tally’ section below.

*** If you do not submit your picks for each round before the puck drops in the first game of each round, you will be disqualified with no reimbursement ***

For more information on current format of the NHL playoffs, please reference the following link:

Point Tally

Quarterfinals

2 points per correct team
2 additional points for correct number of games if correct team chosen in matchup

16 total points for only choosing the correct teams.
32 total points available in the round.

Semifinals

3 points per correct team
3 additional points for correct number of games if correct team chosen in matchup

12 total points for only choosing the correct teams.
24 total points available in the round.

Conference Finals

4 points per correct team
4 additional points for correct number of games if correct team chosen in matchup

8 total points for only choosing the correct teams.
16 total points available in the round.

Final

5 points per correct team
5 additional points for correct number of games if correct team chosen in matchup

5 total points for only choosing the correct team.
10 total points available in the round.

Stanley Cup Champion

You will be given additional points per round for each round that the participants chosen Stanley Cup ‘Champion’ progresses. You will receive the same amount of points each round as received for choosing the correct team (e.g, 2 points in round 1, 3 points in round 2, 4 points in round 3, etc). The points will be awarded at the end of that round. This can result in an additional maximum total of 14 points, with the last 5 points being awarded to participants who chose the correct Stanley Cup ‘Champion’

This will total a maximum of points, with the last 5 points being awarded to participants who chose the correct Stanley Cup ‘Champion’

Opt Out Clause

You can opt to choose against your Stanley Cup ‘Champion’ in any matchup, but if you choose to do so, you forfeit any points for progress each round, starting in and including the round you chose against your ‘Champion’.

Tie Breaker

  1. Correct Stanley Cup ‘Champion’ (if not opted-out)
  2. Stanley Cup ‘Champion’ with furthest progress (up to opt-out if used)
  3. Most Correct Team (and Games if necessary) in Stanley Cup Final
  4. Most Correct Teams and Games Chosen (Both Team and # of Games)
  5. Most Correct Teams Chosen (not including games)

In the rare case of none of the above tie breakers being capable of determining a single 1st, 2nd or 3rd, then the winnings for each placement will be split evenly between the tied competitors for said place.

Rewards

The reward pot will be split amongst the top 3 finishers, with the 3rd place finisher receiving reimbursement of their buy in, and 1st place winning the majority of the pot.
